Call For Participation

PyCon UK is a community conference, made by people like you! All talks and workshops are given by members of the community, and we'd like to invite you to submit a proposal to present a talk or to run a workshop.

To get an idea of topics that people have covered in the past, take a look at last year's schedule. An skeleton of this year's conference already exists.

You don't have to be a high-profile member of the community or a seasoned speaker to submit a proposal. In fact, we're particularly keen to hear from you if this would be your first conference talk. PyCon audiences are always friendly and welcoming.

The Call For Participation for talks and workshops will close on June 19th, and we hope to announce the programme by the beginning of July. When selecting the programme, we will aim to choose sessions that promise to be interesting and enjoyable, while ensuring the line-up is as varied and balanced as possible.

New - Poster Session at PyCon UK

This year we will be adding a poster session to PyCon UK. Poster sessions are great chances to present a topic with the aid of a poster, and to interact with small groups of people in a more informal and relaxed way than a formal talk.

Posters will be accepted for the poster session on a rolling basis until 1 August or the limit of 20 posters is reached. And yes, talk proposals which are not accepted can be reworked and submitted as posters.

For more information about the poster session, visit the poster session page.

Please note that speakers, workshop leaders, and poster presenters are expected to buy tickets, and that we are not able to provide financial assistance to speakers this year.

So, if you'd like to submit a proposal for a talk, a workshop, or a poster please fill out the form below.
